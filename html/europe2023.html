4th of July, 15:40
The day of departure. The train leaves 22:25 from Malmö C. In between heavy showers of rain Suzanne is currently drying her shoes in the sun.

5th and 6th of July in Berlin
We really love Berlin and it seems Berlin loves us back. Despite a fever and swollen sinuses we've explored Kreuzberg and gathered inspiration as well as a desire to come back here soon for a longer stay in the future.





7th, 8th, and 9th of July in Munich and Innsbruck
We left Berlin and headed to Munich. A bit too crowded for Hilma but she had some of the fries from our plant-based currywurst meal by river Isar so she seemed content. Next came the Alps, Tyrol, Innsbruck!






10th of July in Innsbruck
I can't recall much because of food poisoning. I think I watched BBC News on the TV while sipping water.
11th of July in Milano and then in Dogliani until the 17th of July
Milano was a cosy surprise, especially with Caffè Napoli using a lever machine. Dogliani was too good for this world. We didn't see any other tourists or foreigners in this quaint little town. Incredible.








17th to the 21st of July in Nice
Our friend and local buddhist KV joined us from Australia in Nice. He brings the spontaneity.

Moomin
Before we fall asleep every night me and my wife listen to a Moomin audiobook. It is fantastic. There is something about Tove Jansson's writing that touches a certain quality usually only found in poetry. A simplicity I have a difficulty explaining. She can say so much with so little without in any way approaching the complex or pretentious.
